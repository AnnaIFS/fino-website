What It Is

A Program Built for Real Teams

Eight weeks. Small group. Real work situations.

Most teams have the talent. What breaks down is what happens internally when pressure hits. Someone shuts down. Feedback lands wrong. A hard conversation gets avoided for weeks.

Self-leadership Circles give teams a practical framework for noticing those moments and moving through them differently. Using parts work and IFS, people learn to recognize their own patterns and find their way back to clarity in real time.

The language spreads. People start using it in meetings, in feedback conversations, in their 1:1s. That's when things actually shift.

What Changes

The Shift You'll See

  • People stop taking things personally and start getting curious
  • Difficult conversations happen faster and with less damage
  • Leaders notice their reactive patterns before they play out
  • Teams develop a shared language for what's really going on
  • Meetings get shorter. Decisions get clearer. Trust grows.

Each session runs 60 to 90 minutes. We combine teaching moments with live practice, real examples from your team's actual work, and reflection. Sometimes breathwork when the group needs to land before going deeper.

Two facilitators guide every circle. The sessions are dynamic, participative, and grounded in what's actually happening in the room. No lectures. No theory for theory's sake. Just practical work with real situations.

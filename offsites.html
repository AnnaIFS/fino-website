The Experience

Not Your Typical Corporate Retreat

Most offsites are designed around entertainment and logistics. Ours are designed around transformation. We take teams into nature, away from screens, roles, and the patterns that keep them stuck, and create the conditions for something real to happen.

Every element is intentional: the setting, the facilitation, the pacing, the silence. We draw on ancient practices and modern psychology to create experiences that bypass the intellectual and reach the relational.

People leave these experiences changed, not because we told them something new, but because they experienced something they can't unfeel.

“The experiences with Dario and the team helped me connect with what's most important. I felt completely supported. This work gives you clarity on what actually matters and that changes how you show up in everything. I'm still integrating it every day.”
Garrett McNamara, Big Wave Surfer · Guinness World Record Holder · HBO's 100 Foot Wave

What Happens

The Elements

  • Time in nature: forests, mountains, open sky. No conference rooms.
  • Guided inner work using IFS and somatic practices
  • Facilitated conversations that go beneath the surface
  • Shared rituals that build trust and resilience
  • Silence, space, and the kind of rest that actually restores
  • Integration sessions to bring the experience back to daily work

Who It's For

Who It's For

Some teams come because something is broken. Others come because they want to go further than any meeting room has taken them.

  • Leadership teams navigating change or rebuilding trust
  • Founding teams at turning points
  • Groups ready for something real, not another agenda-heavy retreat
  • Anyone curious enough to step outside of the ordinary

Every offsite is custom-designed. We start by understanding your team, your goals, and what you need. Locations range from mountain forests to coastal retreats, always in nature, always away from the everyday.
